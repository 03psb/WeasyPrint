Weasyprint is a free and open source project which is used by a large community of developers around the world. Each developer could become an incredible contributor to support and improve WeasyPrint. We try to answer your questions quickly anytime, anywhere.

So, why donate to WeasyPrint?

2 simples reasons:

  • Our developers would work full-time on your bugs and needs.
  • We could create fantastic new features.

By giving small or large donations, you contribute to create THE WeasyPrint of your dreams!

How to donate?

We have a wonderful page on Patreon, a recurrent donation platforms helping creators and projects to be realized.

Donate: if you agree with our philosophy and our Weasyprint project, help us and donate!
